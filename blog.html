Our Certificate, Locations & Programs for 2023

This year, 2023, we are doing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Graphic Designing, Scratch and MS Suite. We will have trained personnel for all these fields of study to train our participants with all seriousness. Our participants will be selected by Head teachers of the variouse basic schools our participants attend. This process is just to ensure that students are academically cleared to udertake the program.

We will have a class at Sokode-Gbogamein the Volta Region of Ghana and Adabraka-Accrain the Greater Accra Region of Ghana. We are working on expanding to villages like, Abutia-Teti, Agove, and Kloe all in the Volta region of Ghana. After which, Abolove and Nolopi also in the Volta Region of Ghana will experience our presence. We will also be working to open enough centers accross Accra.

We will be giving out Certificates of participation to our students. This certificate is to identify participants as stars who have successfully completed and passed the program. The best Ones who are old enought to teach will be recruited and trained to be part of our facilitators.
